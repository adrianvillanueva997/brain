## 一 (いち / ひと)

| Reading | Word | Meaning |
|---------|------|---------|
| いち | [[一日]] (いちにち) | one day |
| ひと | [[一つ]] (ひとつ) | one (counter) |
| いっ | [[一緒]] (いっしょ) | together |

## 四 (し / よん / よ)

| Reading | Word | Meaning |
|---------|------|---------|
| し | [[四月]] (しがつ) | April |
| よん | [[四人]] (よにん) | four people |
| よっ | [[四つ]] (よっつ) | four (counter) |

> **Note:** し sounds like "death" (死) — so よん is preferred in many contexts.

## 七 (しち / なな)

| Reading | Word | Meaning |
|---------|------|---------|
| しち | [[七月]] (しちがつ) | July |
| なな | [[七つ]] (ななつ) | seven (counter) |
| なな | [[七色]] (なないろ) | seven colors / rainbow |

> **Note:** なな is more common in modern speech; しち is used for months (七月) and time ([七時] しちじ).
