## Textbook

- Genki I (3rd edition) — main textbook
- Genki I Workbook — for practice

## Tools

- **Anki** — spaced repetition (SRS)
  - [Obsidian-to-Anki plugin](https://github.com/ObsidianToAnki/ObsidianToAnki) — syncs `#anki` tagged notes
  - Recommended shared deck: [Genki 1 & 2 Vocabulary](https://ankiweb.net/shared/info/984561954)
- **Jisho** — [jisho.org](https://jisho.org) — dictionary
- **Mazii** — [mazii.net](https://mazii.net) — Japanese-Vietnamese/English dictionary
- **KanjiDamage** — alternative mnemonics for kanji

## Immersion

- Anime with JP subtitles (jimaku.cc or kitsunekko)
- NHK News Easy — [easy NHK news](https://www3.nhk.or.jp/news/easy/)
- Satori Reader — graded reading with audio

## Grammar references

- [Tae Kim's Guide](https://guidetojapanese.org) — free comprehensive grammar
- [Maggie Sensei](https://maggiesensei.com) — conversational grammar explained casually
- [JLPT Sensei](https://jlptsensei.com) — grammar point lists by level
